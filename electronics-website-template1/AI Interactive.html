AI Interactive Display Solution - AI Education Kit

The AI Interactive Display Solution designed by WeeeMake is specially tailored for educational environments such as schools, maker spaces, fablabs, training centers, science museums, and technology centers. These exciting projects provide ideal opportunities for students, makers, and enthusiasts to engage with hands-on and interactive projects.

AI Face Detection

The facial detection control system uses artificial intelligence image recognition to detect the orientation of a person's face and determine the opening and closing of the access control.

Smart Home Assistant

Enable voice commands, voice announcements, and display relevant information such as date, temperature, humidity, light intensity, sound level, changing lighting, and animated actions of sunshine or windows, as well as ventilation and temperature control.

Smart Weather Station

Simulate and display various meteorological parameters such as temperature, humidity, particulate matter, light intensity, UV, wind speed, atmospheric pressure, and rainfall. Equipped with a voice recognition module and a speech synthesis module, it can recognize voice commands and announce the corresponding measured weather parameters.

Gesture-control Sokoban Game

The system involves game recognition allowing users to control a Sokoban game using hand gestures.

Racing Seesaw

This interactive project combines mechanics and motion. Users can control and manipulate the seesaw to create a competitive racing game.

Temperature Measurement

By placing your palm above the probe, the infrared thermometer will measure body temperature and announce it.
