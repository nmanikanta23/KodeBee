AI Factory Robot Kit - AI Robot Education Series

Model: 160556

AI Factory Robot Kit is a project-based demostration robot kit for AI image recognition education. It is a set of tools and materials that can be used to build and program a robot with artificial intelligence capabilities. These kits typically include components such as sensors, motors, and microcontrollers, as well as software for programming the robot's movements and behaviors. The goal is to provide a fun and interactive way for people to learn about AI and robotics.

This kit uses ELF AIOT K210 mainboard as the controller, includes three projects - the AI factory gate, the warehouse robot, and the AI sorting robot. It simulates the common scenes of AI factories. By assembly, program and control, students get hands-on experience on programming, robotics, AI education and industry 4.0.
