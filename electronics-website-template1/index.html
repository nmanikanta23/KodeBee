    KODEBEE BRAND STORY

    In July 2017, KodeBee was founded in Shenzhen, Nanshan. We try to make our own metal robot building platform for STEM education. KodeBee is a brand under which the KodeBee Co., Ltd. was created by three founders, each one represents an “e” in the brand KodeBee.

    The company offers a compatible platform with all kinds of open-source electronic and programming platforms to build win-win cooperation in the STEAM education hardware system.

    About Us

    KodeBee is a metal educational robot DIY platform for kids 8+ to professional level to learn robotics, programming, AI, IoT, etc. We are a comprehensive STEAM education solution platform integrating R&D, production, and sales.

    Our Vision

    Build the next generation DIY platform, inspire children's passion and dream of creation. Popularize robot education, programming education, and AI education.

    Cooperation

    KodeBee offers a turnkey solution of AI and STEAM robot classroom solution with themes of robotics, programming, AI, IoT, etc. We develop mechanical parts, electronics, software, and curriculum in one station by ourselves. Welcome to contact us for further cooperation and customization!
