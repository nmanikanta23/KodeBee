4WD Mecanum Chassis Robot Education Kit for micro:bit / mPython Board

An expansion set for micro:bit V2 and mPython board. The product is equipped with up to 10+ electronic modules and can assemble 11 robot projects including a four-wheel drive moving robot chassis for rich robot applications.

Contact Us Quick Inquiry

Details

Weeemake 4WD Mecanum Chassis Robot Education Kit for micro:bit/mPython board is an expansion set for micro:bit V2 and mPython board. The shield is compatible with both controllers, with 4 dc motor ports, 4 RJ11 ports, 2 servo motor ports, an IR receiver, and 1 pc of 18650 lithium battery. The product is equipped with up to 10+ electronic modules and can assemble 11 robot projects including a four-wheel drive moving robot chassis for rich robot applications.

Note: the micro:bit or mPython board is not included in the kit, and should be purchased separately.

Projects

2 in 1 Shield Board for micro:bit and mPython board

ELF 2 IN 1 Shield Board is an expansion board which supports micro:bit V1, micro:bit V2 and mPython board.

This shield board comes with one controller slot, 4 x RJ11 port (Weeemake sensors), 4 x DC motor ports, 2 x servo motor ports, 1 x IR receiver, 1 x 18650 rechargeable Li-ion battery, and 1 x microUSB port for battery charging.

Simple but powerful to drive a 4WD robot chassis, or more projects.
